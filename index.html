Game Instructions

  • 🕹 On PC, use W A S D to move your character.
  • 🕴 Press Space to interact with objects, NPCs, and during tasks.
  • Press M to Mute/Unmute the sound system
  • Press ↑ to increase Volume
  • Press ↓ to decrease Volume
  • 🖱 Use your Mouse for performing tasks like puzzles or games.
  • 📱 On mobile, use the virtual joystick to move and tap the Action button to interact.
  • 🔑 Collect 6 keys to unlock the hidden Underground Map.
  • 🎰 Earn points through the Spin Wheel after collecting every 2 keys.
  • 💸 Redeem collected points for real cash rewards
  • 🗣 Interact with NPCs to receive clues, story quests, and bonus hints.
  • 🚪 Solve puzzles and discover secret paths to progress further.
  • 📜 Check your key count and point score using the top-right status counters.
  • 🏠 Tap the Home button anytime to return to the main screen.
  • 🗝 In the Underground Map, guess the Treasure Unlock Code 2 times per day.
  • 🤝 Get extra chances by referring friends — more friends, more guesses!
